About the Project

The architecture, dataset, and ML pipeline behind TruthShield.

How It Works

TruthShield analyzes written content for misinformation signals, sensationalism, and unreliable language patterns.

Headline Mode — trained on short news titles. Accuracy: 92.29%
Article Mode — evaluates full article bodies. Accuracy: 93.50%

ML Pipeline

1. Preprocessing

Removes special characters, tokenizes, strips stop words, and lemmatizes to root forms.

2. Vectorization

TF-IDF converts word frequency into numerical weight vectors.

3. Classification

Passive-Aggressive Classifier flags unreliable content with a high-dimensional boundary.
